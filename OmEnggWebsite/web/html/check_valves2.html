Description
Flutec High Pressure Non Return Valve (NRV) also known as High Pressure Check Valve is designed as per German Technology for one directional flow regulation. Flutec team has specialized themselves in High Pressure application and so conventionally available non return valve in almost all major store cannot be the substitute of Flutec Non Return Valves (NRV). High Pressure application can be dangerous in case of failure. Flutec High Pressure Non Return Valves (NRV) / Check Valve are satisfactorily working in many locations in world.  This compact design High Pressure Non Return Valve (NRV / Check Valve) are manufactured for High Pressure up to 1000bar (15000PSI) & have huge application in High Pressure Hydraulic Systems.
Features of Flutec High Pressure Non Return Valves (NRV) / Check Valve:
  • Working pressure up to 1000 bar (Standard Range 500 bar / 7500 PSI)..
  • Working temperature – 20oC to + 250oC
  • Flow up to 600 LPM
  • Individual pressure testing ensures safety.
  • Hexagonal housing available in carbon steel phosphate plated.
  • Other metallic finish on request.
  • Are also manufactured in stainless steel & Brass material.
  • Manifold Block Mounting Non Return Valves / Check Valve also can be supplied
